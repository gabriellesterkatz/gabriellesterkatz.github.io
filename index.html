Gabe Katz

Drummer & Composer

Born in Los Angeles CA, he started drumming at age 3. He studied with Alex Cline until the age of 7 when he moved to Sebastopol CA. He grew up playing in bands such as The Coma Lilies, Shat, Sex Goddess, and Dos Gringos. At age 21 he moved to Portland OR. There, he played in My Dads, Horders, Mean Girls, Shat Duo, Jaun Profit Organization, Dallas County Potential, Lil Pimp, Hot Pink, Hosannas, and The Sarcastic Dharma Society.
